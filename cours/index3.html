<h3>Motion Sickness</h3>
<div class="r-stretch">
<div style="min-height: 50px;"></div>
<p style="text-decoration: underline;">Motion sickness -- overview</p>
<ul style="font-size: 30pt;">
<li>Experienced by a large portion of people: ~25%</li>
<li>Resulting from sensory conflict between what we see and what we feel</li>
<li>Primarily due to lateral motion</li>
<li>It is an unsolved problem (both for research and spread of VR)</li>
</ul>
</div>
</section>
<section data-auto-animate data-transition="fade-in fade-out">
<h3>Motion Sickness</h3>
<div class="r-stretch">
<div style="min-height: 50px;"></div>
<p style="text-decoration: underline;">Motion sickness -- causes</p>
<ul style="font-size: 30pt;">
<li>Equipment: 3 DoF vs 6 DoF, controller type</li>
<li>Latency: major reason of motion sickness</li>
<li>Postural instability (due to visual movement)</li>
<li>Biological factors: gender, age, health</li>
</ul>
